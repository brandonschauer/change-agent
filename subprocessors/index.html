Sub-processors

Last updated: July 15, 2026

We use the following sub-processors to provide Change Agent. Each processes personal data only on our instructions, under a data-processing agreement, and only as needed for its stated purpose. Region/transfer details should be confirmed against each provider's current documentation and your account configuration.

Notes

  • No training on user data: providers are operated in no-training configurations (routing configured to exclude training endpoints; primary model under enterprise terms). See the Privacy Policy §3.
  • Encryption: all transfers over TLS; data encrypted at rest (AES-256 in Neo4j Aura / Supabase; encryption-at-rest attested under LangChain's SOC 2 Type II).
  • Cross-border transfers: US-located sub-processors are covered by SCCs and/or EU-US Data Privacy Framework certification — tracked in DPA_SCC_TRACKER.md.
  • This list is maintained as sub-processors change; subscribe at https://forms.microsoft.com/r/QWP6JcXRK2 for advance notice.
