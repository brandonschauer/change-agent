Sub-processors

Last updated: July 20, 2026

We use the following sub-processors to provide Change Agent. Each processes personal data only on our instructions, under a data-processing agreement (see status below), and only as needed for its stated purpose. Region/transfer details should be confirmed against each provider's current documentation and your account configuration.

Notes

  • No training on user data: providers are operated in no-training configurations (routing configured to exclude training endpoints; primary model under enterprise terms). See the Privacy Policy §3.
  • Retention at model providers: the retention shown for each model provider is that provider's own published policy as reported by OpenRouter, accurate as of the date above. Provider policies can change without notice to us; we re-check them when this list is reviewed.
  • Encryption: all transfers over TLS; data encrypted at rest (AES-256 in Neo4j Aura / Supabase; encryption-at-rest attested under LangChain's SOC 2 Type II).
  • Cross-border transfers: US-located sub-processors are covered by SCCs and/or EU-US Data Privacy Framework certification — tracked in DPA_SCC_TRACKER.md.
  • This list is maintained as sub-processors change; subscribe at https://forms.microsoft.com/r/QWP6JcXRK2 for advance notice.
